At LivePlan³, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our app and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name and email address during account creation
  • Financial data you enter (e.g., income, expenses, goals)
  • Optional feedback or support messages

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To personalize your experience and communicate with you
  • To analyze usage patterns for internal development

3. Data Security

We use secure encryption, token-based authentication, and industry-standard cloud infrastructure to keep your data safe.

4. Sharing of Information

We never sell your data. Your information is only shared with trusted third-party providers when necessary to operate the service, or when required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, edit, or delete your data via your account
  • Request data export or complete deletion
  • Contact us directly at support@liveplan3.com for any privacy-related requests

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use limited tracking tools (e.g., analytics) to improve app performance and usability. No financial or sensitive data is used for marketing or advertising.

7. Legal Basis

We process your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws (including GDPR, where applicable), based on your consent and the necessity of providing our services.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of major changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes your acceptance.

If you have any questions or requests, please contact us at support@liveplan3.com
