About Us

Our Story

LivePlan³ was born from a simple but powerful idea: that everyone deserves the ability to take control of their finances — easily, visually, and with purpose.

In a world full of complex spreadsheets and confusing finance apps, we set out to build something different — a clean, modern, and user-friendly platform that helps people stay on top of their income, expenses, and future goals without the overwhelm.

Our Mission

To empower a new generation of financially confident individuals by making budgeting and financial planning simple, intuitive, and actionable.
